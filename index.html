项目经历 (Projects)

可信学习型优化 (Trustworthy Learning-Based Optimization)

研究如何将优化算法转化为可解释、可控制、可验证的学习型求解器， 并结合约束结构、反馈控制与原始-对偶残差设计提升算法可靠性。

I study how optimization algorithms can be transformed into interpretable, controllable, and verifiable learning-based solvers by integrating constraint structures, feedback control, and primal-dual residual design.

能源系统调度与决策 (Energy System Scheduling)

研究面向新能源系统、储能系统和综合能源系统的优化调度方法， 关注不确定性条件下的可靠运行与约束决策。

I work on optimization and learning methods for renewable energy scheduling, storage coordination, integrated energy systems, and constrained decision-making under uncertainty.
